OPEN ALL 10 CELLS

You reach to open all the cells, you are not sure why you acted out of order but something in you knew that it was the right choice. Maybe the chaos following will allow for a quicker escape.

You arrive at the entrance of the cells to see 10 scared and frail girls, their ages range from toddler to teen. You quickly locate TARGET #9152.

A voice behind you softly mumbles a name. You turn in disbelief, as you realize who the voice came from. You have not heard your real name since your family was murdered...
