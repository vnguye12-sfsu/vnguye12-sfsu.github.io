MISSION START

You arrive on scene behind HRVST's research headquarters, you notice that the back door has minimal security. You have your knife unsheathed.

“Killing the guards is the faster way” you think to yourself, “however, I risk the bodies being found. If I am patient enough I can easily sneak past them as they come in and out of the facility.”
