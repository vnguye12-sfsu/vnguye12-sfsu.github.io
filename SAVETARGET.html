SAVE TARGET ONLY

You push aside your emotions and decide to follow the mission orders. You think to yourself, “If she is alive now, she will survive longer. I can come back and save her.” You turn back around to retrieve TARGET #9152, she's only about 4 years of age.

You quickly escort the terrified target back up to ground level up the emergency stairs. The alarms start to ring, it seems like someone noticed there has been an intruder.

You can hear hundreds of foot steps moving within the facility. Luckily, your quick decision making skills has given you the upperhand. You see the exit.
